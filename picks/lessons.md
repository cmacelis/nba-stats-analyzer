## Rules (self-imposed based on results)

1. **Downgrade consensus road favorites** — if 3+ expert sources agree on a road spread, treat it as moderate confidence, not high.
2. **Never base a fade on emotional narratives alone** — require supporting statistical evidence (pace, efficiency, rest days) not just "they got blown out."
3. **Totals > spreads early on** — until spread picks prove themselves, lean into totals where pace/defense models have edge.
4. **Player props need game-script check** — consider whether the player's team is likely to be ahead/behind and how that affects their usage pattern.
5. **Max 4 picks per day** — fewer, higher-quality picks. "No pick" is always valid.
6. **Every pick needs an edge_type** — if you can't name the specific statistical edge (rest, pace, defense, sharp money), don't make the pick. "Consensus" alone is NOT an edge (0-4 Day 1).
7. **Rest/B2B is king** — always check schedule context. Team on B2B vs. rested opponent = strongest available edge.
8. **Unit sizing by confidence** — low = 0.5u, moderate = 1u, high = 1.5u (max). No 2u plays until record exceeds 55%.
